About ITELF

iTELF, is the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT which provides a dais for upcoming IT Entrepreneurs and Leaders to explore the world of start-ups, exchange ideas, foster and enhance their leadership skills.

About SproutIT

Every new idea begins with a thought and a voice that motivates you to go the distance and achieve your goals inspite of the innumerable hurdles along the way. Keeping this spirit in mind, we at SCIT present SproutIT, an event which is designed to bring out the leader in you and lend a ear to that entrepreneurial inner voice.
